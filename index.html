Some things I like compiled in a website to showcase links and images.

Wikipedia

The Free Encyclopedia

Wikipedia is an amazing website that had an encyclopedic entry for nearly anything you can think of.
Its content is user-generated, but every new page and edit is checked by a "Chief Editor"
befor it goes public. Although not infaluable, Wikipedia pages are generaly safe from
blatantly false information and extreme biases.

Yellowstone

National Park

Yellowstone is the most incredible place on the planet. Its geothermal features are so
unique that when it was first discovered, no one believed it was real. Even the newspapers
refused to report about a land of "Rainbow lakes, boiling mud pudles, and geysers of water
over 200 feet high" because "we don't report fiction."

Mario Party

Superstars

I was skeptical at first, but Mario Party Superstars (Nintendo Switch, 2021) ended up being
on of the most fun party-multiplayer games I have ever played. Across 5 board and 105 minigames,
you won't run out of content for an entire play session. Plus, in addition to local multiplayer,
it also has, in a rare move from Nintendo, good online.
